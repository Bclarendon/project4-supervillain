Neuros' superpowers include the ability to control artificial inteligence and merging his body with technology. All technology from computers to smartphones 'speak' to him. After mastering his ability he quickly developed an advanced A.I. system, which was leagues ahead anything the world had ever seen. Neuros was able to quickly patented the Neuron project in conjunction with the corporation. The little known secret to the A.I. success is that Neuros was able to program parts of himself into the system, therefore acting as an omnipresent force in it's development.

Neuros also sports a robot exo-skeleton for instances when the local superhero tries to stop him. Try as they might...
