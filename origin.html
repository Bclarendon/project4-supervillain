The Neuron Coporation is a globally reaching technology empire lead by supervillain and philanthropist Neuros. The coporation functions as a business operation front for Neuros' evil empire. Since it's debut on the open market, speculation continues to rise about ties to the villain Neuros. The veiled intentions of the corporation and where its controversial A.I technology is leading, forced the corporation's second identity.

Neuros started as a anti-establishment hacker, but realized the world was too corrupt for saving. Since then he has vowed to put society under the heel of his tyranny to save humanity from what he states as 'a point of no return'.
